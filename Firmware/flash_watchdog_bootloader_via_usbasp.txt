1. connect usbasp with arduino nano
2. plug usbasp to usb port
3. plug arduino nano to usb port
4. execute
        avrdude -c usbasp -p m328p -P usb -b 115200 -U flash:w:optiboot_atmega328.hex:i
5. unplug usbasp from usb port
6. unplug arduino nano from usb port
7. disconnect usbasp from arduino nano
8. plug arduino nano to usb port
9. execute
        avrdude -c arduino -p m328p -P /dev/ttyUSB0 -b 115200 -U flash:w:firmware.hex:a
10. do not forget to replace diode D1 on the nano board with a 0R resistor or a 600mA polyfuse
